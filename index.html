About Me

From analyzing large datasets to deploying AI-powered applications, my journey has been about making data work smarter and more impactful. I’m passionate about Large Language Models (LLMs), Data Analytics, and AI solutions that go beyond numbers—tools that help people ask better questions, uncover deeper insights, and drive informed decision-making.

I’ve developed projects such as a NYC Property Price Predictor using PySpark, XGBoost, and SHAP, and a Streamlit app for LLMs in Business Intelligence powered by Hugging Face transformers, gaining hands-on experience with predictive modeling, natural language query generation, and interactive analytics. Along the way, I’ve worked with models and algorithms including Linear Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, and Decision Trees to solve business and analytical challenges.

I’m continuing to expand my expertise in LLMs, NLP, and AI model deployment, while exploring emerging areas such as Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), embeddings, and multimodal AI. My focus is on bridging advanced AI techniques with real-world applications to create solutions that are both powerful and practical.
